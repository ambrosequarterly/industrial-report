  • Every January 10, April 10, July 10, and October 10 at 8am ET, GitHub automatically checks CBRE's website for new quarterly Industrial Figures reports across all 11 markets
  • One summary email is sent listing which markets have new reports available and which are still pending
  • Subject line: "Ambrose Quarterly Report Available - [Month] [Year]"
  • The email contains a direct link to this website

Markets with Full Submarket Breakdown

All 11 markets include a full submarket-level data table with vacancy, availability, asking rent, net absorption, and under construction data:

  • Indianapolis, IN — 6 submarkets: CBD, East, Northeast, Northwest, South, Southwest
  • Denver, CO — 5 submarkets: Northeast/I-70, Southeast/I-25, Northwest/Arvada, Southwest/Jefferson, North Central
  • Orlando / Central FL — 5 submarkets: Airport/OIA, I-4 West, Northwest/Apopka, Northeast/Sanford, South/Osceola
  • Miami / South FL — Miami-Dade County and Broward County with separate market data
  • Chicago, IL — 6 submarkets: O'Hare, I-80/Joliet, North I-55, DuPage County, South Suburbs, Lake County
  • Phoenix, AZ — 5 submarkets: Southwest/Tolleson, Southeast/Mesa, Airport/Sky Harbor, Deer Valley, East Valley
  • Columbus / Central OH — 5 submarkets: Southeast/Rickenbacker, Southwest, Pickaway County, Northeast/Intel, Northwest
  • Cleveland / NE Ohio — 5 submarkets: Airport/West, East/Solon, Southeast, South/Streetsboro, Downtown
  • Cincinnati, OH — 5 submarkets: Northeast/Mason, Northwest/Fairfield, Airport/CVG, Central, Southeast/Clermont
  • Louisville, KY — 5 submarkets: Airport/UPS Worldport, Northeast, South/I-65, Northwest/Riverport, Oldham County
  • Nashville, TN — 5 submarkets: Airport/BNA, Southeast/Antioch, Lebanon/Mt. Juliet, North/Gallatin, West/Clarksville

When You Get the Email

  • Open claude.ai and paste in the quarterly update prompt from the handoff guide
  • Paste the current index.html at the bottom — Claude finds all new CBRE reports automatically
  • Paste Claude's updated index.html into GitHub — the website updates within 60 seconds
